ST JOSEPH TECHNICAL INSTITUTE FOR THE DEAF

MISSION

The mission statement of St. Joseph Technical Institute for the Deaf is To equip learners with hearing impairment, with technical knowledge, skills and attitude for self-reliance

VISSION

The vision statement of St. Joseph Technical Institute for the Deaf is - To be a global centre for modern technology for learners with emphasis on learners with hearing impairment.
Core Values
St. Joseph’s Technical Institute for the Deaf is a service delivery institution driven by the following core values: Professionalism, Integrity and Honesty, Tolerance and Empathy, Responsibility, Peace and Unity, Respect and Courtesy.
